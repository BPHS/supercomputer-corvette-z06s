Repositories

Our first repository contains a program that runs loop. You can pick one of four options to choose from. The options are: 1.) Count to 1000000 from 1 by 1s 2.) Count to 1000000 from 1 by 5s 3.) Count down to 1 from 100 by 1s 4.) Count to 10000 from 1000 by 10's. So, go ahead and give one a try.
